About the Artist

Ingrid Amuri Rutherford is a visual artist, graphic designer and art instructor based in Son, Vestby — south of Oslo, Norway. She runs Paint & Fun Norway, offering creative art experiences and instruction.

An active force in the local art scene, Ingrid is a key contributor to Vestby Kunstforening (Vestby Art Association), where she helps organize exhibitions, art walks, and community events that bring artists and audiences together.

Her own work spans a range of media and scales — from intimate pieces to large-format paintings. Commissions are welcome. Get in touch to discuss a custom piece.

Artistic Vision

My art explores the relationship between nature, light, and emotion. Inspired by the Norwegian landscape and my roots, my work strives to capture the moments of beauty that surround us in everyday life.
